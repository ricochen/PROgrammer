Learn how to code from beginner to a little bit better than beginner, the right way.

Too often, people who are learning to code, finish a resource and don't know what to learn next. This app will provide a guideline, and steer people in the right direction by only giving one resource at a time. After they finish a resource, they will be provided a test to see if they truly understand the material, if they pass they will be provided the next resource to learn.
